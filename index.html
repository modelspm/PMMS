What we offer

We offer customized miniature models to private clients. It can be a souvenir, a gift, or anything else you need, in miniature scale. We improve the product with personalized feedback which makes it harder for you to be unsatisfied with our service.

We provide high-quality presentation setups using miniatures for building proposals and real estate development. As our team is made up of professionals with years of experience in the architecture field, we can guarantee a top-tier outcome.

The best part of our service is our specialization in making miniatures for exhibitions and museums. We provide realistic, intricate, and handcrafted models which can also follow a research component to get the outcome you desire.
